ALLERGY TO LEGUMES

6 Months to 1.5 Years

Symptoms and Diagnosis

Cross-Reactivity:

  • Other Allergens: Be vigilant for potential reactions to other common allergens like peanuts or soy, as they can sometimes cause cross-reactive responses.
  • Food Diaries: Maintain a detailed food diary to help identify and track potential cross-reactive foods.

Family History:

  • Genetic Counseling: Consider genetic counseling if there is a significant family history of food allergies, as this can provide insight into potential risks and management strategies.

Legume-Free Diet

Introduce Alternatives Gradually:

  • Varied Iron Sources: Besides fortified cereals and lean meats, consider introducing iron-rich vegetables like spinach and sweet potatoes, ensuring they're prepared safely for the child's age.
  • Protein-Rich Snacks: Offer age-appropriate snacks like small pieces of chicken, fish, or tofu to ensure adequate protein intake.
