Digestive age in children?

Welcome to our dedicated section on indigestion issues related to dairy, wheat, and legume intolerances in children aged from 1 month to 10 years. Understanding and managing these sensitivities early on is crucial for your child's health and comfort. Whether your child is dealing with lactose intolerance, gluten sensitivity, or a reaction to legumes, our goal is to provide you with practical advice, nutritional guidance, and support to ensure they lead a healthy and happy life. Explore our resources to learn how to navigate these challenges effectively.
