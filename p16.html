1.Home-Cooked Options:

  • Allergy-Friendly Recipes: Develop a collection of allergy-friendly recipes that avoid legumes and other potential allergens. Consider creating a recipe book or resource guide for easy reference.
  • Safe Kitchen Practices: Implement strict kitchen practices to avoid cross-contact with legumes. Use separate utensils and cooking surfaces for legume-free foods.

Management Tips

Label Reading:

  • Allergen Alerts: Familiarize yourself with common allergens listed on food labels, including alternative names for legumes (e.g., "pulse").
  • Hidden Ingredients: Learn about hidden sources of legumes in processed foods, such as in thickeners, emulsifiers, or natural flavorings.

Travel Preparedness:

  • Emergency Kit: Prepare an emergency kit that includes not just medication but also a copy of the allergy action plan and emergency contact numbers.
  • Safe Food Options: Research and plan for safe food options in advance when traveling. Pack non-perishable, legume-free snacks to avoid reliance on local food sources.
