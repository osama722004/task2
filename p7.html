Dairy-Free Food Alternatives for Babies

Introducing Solid Foods:

Dairy-Free Cereals:

  • Gluten-Free Oats: A nutritious option suitable for babies with dairy allergies.
  • Rice Cereal: Easy to digest and a safe choice for young babies.

Fruits:

  • Mashed Apples: Rich in vitamins and a safe, nutritious choice.
  • Bananas: Provide quick energy and are easy to digest for babies.
  • Avocado: Packed with healthy fats and helps in growth and development.

Vegetables:

  • Mashed Carrots: A good source of beta-carotene and vitamin A.
  • Mashed Sweet Potatoes: Nutritious and high in fiber.
  • Mashed Zucchini: Gentle on the stomach and easy to digest.

Plant-Based Proteins:

  • Red Lentils: A rich source of protein and fiber that can be cooked until soft.
  • Mashed Green Peas: Full of protein and vitamins, making them a good option for babies.

Additional Tips:

  • Food Variety: Ensure a wide range of foods to provide all essential nutrients.
  • Monitor Allergies: Introduce one type of food at a time and wait 3-5 days to observe any allergic reactions before offering another.
